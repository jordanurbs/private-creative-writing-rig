Create a new writing project from a template.

Ask the writer:
1. What type of project? (novel, short-story, essay, blog, screenplay, poetry-collection)
2. What is the title?

Then copy the matching template from `templates/<type>/` to `projects/<title-slug>/`.

Replace `{{title}}` with the project title and `{{date}}` with today's date in all files.

After creating the project, open the main writing file (first chapter, story file, etc.) and tell the writer they are ready to start. Mention they can use `/project:style` after writing a few pages to generate a style guide.
