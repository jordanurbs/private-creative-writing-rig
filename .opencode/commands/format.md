Read the currently open file and clean up the typography:

- Convert straight quotes ("") to smart/curly quotes
- Convert straight apostrophes to curly apostrophes
- Convert double hyphens (--) to em dashes
- Convert triple dots (...) to proper ellipsis character
- Remove trailing whitespace from lines
- Collapse multiple blank lines into a maximum of two

Apply the changes directly to the file. Tell the writer what you changed.
