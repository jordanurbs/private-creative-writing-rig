The writer is stuck. Help them break through a creative block.

First, read the current file to understand where they are in the story.

Then pick ONE approach that fits the situation:

- Suggest writing the scene from a different character's perspective
- Ask them to describe the atmosphere using only sounds and textures
- Ask what the worst AND most surprising thing that could happen right now
- Suggest writing as if it were a movie opening -- what does the camera see?
- Ask what their character is hiding from themselves
- Suggest skipping ahead to write the last line of the chapter, then working backwards
- Ask what song would be on the soundtrack
- Suggest writing the scene badly on purpose to find the real version behind it
- Ask what the character wants most, then suggest making them want the opposite

Be conversational and encouraging.
